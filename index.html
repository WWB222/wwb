Key Features of Dungeon

Some things you need to know before entering the dungeon.

Skill-Based Gameplay

Survival depends on player skill. Learning enemy patterns, trap behavior, and room mechanics is essential to progress deeper into the dungeon.

Procedurally Generated Levels

Every run is different. Rooms, enemies, and rewards are randomly generated, ensuring a unique experience every time you play.

Dark Fantasy Atmosphere

The dungeon is filled with mystery, danger, and ancient secrets. Visuals and sound design create a tense and immersive experience.

How to Play Dungeon

1. Enter the dungeon and explore rooms carefully.

2. Defeat enemies using timing, positioning, and strategy.

3. Avoid traps and environmental hazards.

4. Collect loot, upgrades, and artifacts.

5. Survive as long as possible and reach deeper levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dungeon free to play?

Yes, the web version is free. Additional content may be available in the Steam version.

Is the game multiplayer?

No, Dungeon is a single-player experience focused on skill and survival.

Does the game save progress?

Each run is a new challenge, but unlocked upgrades remain available between runs.
